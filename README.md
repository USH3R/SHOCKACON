## 🛡️ Project Overview
**SHOCKACON** (Systemic Holistic Oversight for Cognitive Analysis & Content Networking) is a defensive framework designed to neutralize deceptive AI-generated content. It identifies misinformation, deepfake scams, and adversarial influence campaigns in real-time.

### 🧠 The SHOCKACON Engine
* **Python (The Sentinel):** Machine learning models that perform sentiment and linguistic analysis to flag "Shock-Value" disinformation.
* **Java (The Federal Liaison):** Implements **NIST SP 800-161** (Supply Chain Risk Management) to verify the origin and provenance of digital messages.
* **C++ (The Secure Enclave):** Low-latency packet inspection to detect bot-farm signatures and automated campaign patterns.

### 📉 Risk Assessment Formula
SHOCKACON calculates the **Deception Index ($\Delta$)** based on source reliability and content volatility:
$$\Delta = \frac{\sum (V_w \times A_s)}{\text{Source\_Trust}}$$
Where $V_w$ is the volatility weight and $A_s$ is the anomaly score of the media metadata.
